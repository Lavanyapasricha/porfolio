About

swipe for more →

I design things that look good and work even better.

I'm a UI/UX designer with a graphic design background, so I care just as much about the pixels as the people using them. Leading as the Design Head of my college's esports club taught me how to build strong visual identities, collaborate under pressure, and ship creative work fast.

I enjoy turning messy problems into simple, intuitive experiences and obsessing over the details no one notices until they're missing. Fueled by curiosity, endless iterations, and an unhealthy amount of coffee & diet coke :))
